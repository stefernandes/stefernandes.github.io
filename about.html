About me

Eric Niu

Welcome to my page, where I exhibit a selection of my photographic and architectural works.

My name is Eric, I am currently an architecture student at the École Nationale Supérieure d'Architecture de Paris la Villette (ENSAPLV).

Traveling is very important to me, it's moments that generate memorable experiences, which bring me a lot of inspiration.  Photography and retouching allow me to capture and experience these inspirations and to share them.

I also take advantage of this platform to share my notable architecture projects that I have completed during my studies.

This site is made possible thanks to the help of my friend Stéphane Pacheco Fernandes.
